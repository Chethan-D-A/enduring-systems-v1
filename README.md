# enduring-systems
How do enduring value-generating systems come into existence and continue existing beyond their creator?

---

# 1. What is this repository?
This repository documents my attempts to investigate difficult questions through short, deadline-constrained research projects.

# 2. What question is being investigated?
How do enduring value-generating systems come into existence and continue existing beyond their creator?
This question has fascinated me for years, and this repository documents my attempts to investigate it systematically.

# 3. Why does the question matter?
This question matters because the humanity has always advanced through value generating systems that lived long after its creator is gone. This question explores on how, why and what makes some systems continue existing beyond its creator while others gradually go out of existence.

# 4. What is the process used?
I have used the process of collecting the sources, reading and making 5 line points for each case studies, find a common pattern in them, take top 6 common patterns, attack those very patterns with questions, and arrive at a version 1 conclusion about which might be the reasons.
Here the sources are strictly constrained to 20 as this topic has a tendency to drag humans into endless deep dives through an ocean of interconnected fields.

# 5. What are the project rules?
For this project, rules are hard and immutable. It is the version 1 of the projects. The rules are:
1. Hard constraint on the sources - 20 ONLY
2. A hard deadline of 6 days to ensure no topic drift but ample time for version 1
