# Project Rules

Question:
How do enduring value-generating systems come into existence and continue existing
beyond their creator?

Duration:
6 days total.

Hard Deadline:
Publish at the end of Day 6.
No extensions.
No exceptions.

Research:
Stop gathering new sources at the end of Day 4.
Writing
Day 5 and Day 6 are primarily synthesis and writing.

Publication:
Substack, GitHub, PDF, personal website-doesn't matter.
The artifact must exist publicly or at least be archived as a finished version.
